Our Story

Celebrating the Hands That Shape India

CRAFT BAZAAR was born from a single belief — that the hands of an artisan carry centuries of wisdom, and that wisdom deserves to reach the world.

Why we exist

India has over 7 million artisans. Most earn less than ₹200 a day.

Despite India being the world's largest producer of handicrafts, the people who create them rarely benefit from the value they generate. Middlemen, lack of digital access, and an absence of authentic storytelling leave artisans invisible.

CRAFT BAZAAR removes that distance. We connect artisans directly with buyers who genuinely care about what they're buying — and why it matters.

The Process

How CRAFT BAZAAR Works

🧶

Artisan Lists a Craft

An artisan signs up, creates their profile, and lists their handmade products with photos, descriptions, pricing — and their own craft story.

✅

Verified Handmade

Products go through our Verified Handmade process — artisans fill a craft questionnaire, and genuine pieces earn a trust badge visible to buyers.

🛒

Customer Discovers & Buys

Buyers explore by state, category, or search — find a piece they love, read its story, and purchase securely with delivery to their door.

⭐

Stories & Reviews

Buyers leave reviews, artisans build reputation, and each transaction keeps a traditional craft alive for one more generation.

What we believe in

Our Values

🏛️

Heritage First

Every craft tradition on our platform has roots that go back centuries. We treat that heritage with reverence, not as a marketing strategy.

🤝

Fair to Artisans

Artisans set their own prices. We never undercut their work by suggesting "discounts." Their skill has value, and we protect that.

🛡️

Radical Transparency

Our Verified Handmade badge is earned, not paid for. We show buyers exactly who made something, where it came from, and how it was made.
